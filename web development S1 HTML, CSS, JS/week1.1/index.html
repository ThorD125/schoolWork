Applied Computer Sciences at HOWEST

A passion for creating programmes that help people!

The future is digital and we are already experiencing that today. Our passion is developing digital solutions that help shape the future.

Websites, apps, embedded devices, … Software comes in many forms and is constantly evolving. Almost all of them are designed using the same set of basic principles. You will gradually learn this basis during the programme.

You will also gain insight into the systems and technologies that make this possible.

And last but not least, the human component is also covered. You learn how to work in a team, in dialogue with customers and end users.

In short, a contemporary education that will prepare you for a successful career within a domain that is evolving at lightning speed.

Why study at HOWEST?

In Applied Computer Sciences, there are four study fields to specialise in:

  • in software- and system developement (SOFTWARE ENGINEER)
  • in counteracting digital piracy (CYBER SECURITY)
  • in measuring up companies' needs and meeting them with existing software solutions (ICT & Blockchain CONSULTANT)
  • in leveraging the knowledge of big data (AI Business Professional)

The 5 principal topics

Applied Computer Sciences is comprised of 5 principal topics. Below, they are listed chronologically:

  1. ICT INFRASTRUCTURE
  2. INFORMATION MANAGEMENT
  3. SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT
  4. ICT-MANAGEMENT
  5. WEB AND MOBIELE TECHNOLOGY

International

Wat to study abroad? Perhaps an international internship is more your forté? Howest values international endeavours and stimulates all students to take on adventure. In the fourth semester, you'll be introduced to a mandatory international experience, where you'll work together with students from all over the world.
